FAQ

Common Questions

How does time-based pricing work? +
The faster you need your clothes, the higher the rate. Express (2hr) costs more than Next Day. You choose based on urgency and budget.
Is there a minimum order value? +
Yes, minimum order is ₹199. For laundry, minimum weight is 2 kg.
Do you offer pickup at specific times? +
Yes! You can select your preferred pickup slot — morning (7–11am), afternoon (12–4pm), or evening (5–9pm).
What if my clothes are damaged? +
All garments are insured. In the unlikely event of damage, we provide full compensation as per our garment protection policy.
Are pickup and delivery charges extra? +
Pickup & delivery is FREE on orders above ₹499. Below that, a nominal ₹49 delivery fee applies.
